As Game & Level Designer

I believe that becoming a game designer requires constantly enhancing one's creativity and imagination to design unique and captivating gameplay. Game designers also necessitates interaction with players to understand their needs and feedback, continuously improving and creating satisfying gaming experiences.

Therefore, during my undergraduate studies, I chose a major in interactive media and pursued game design as a minor, while selecting film production as my second major. I believe that the creative and storytelling techniques of film production can also be applied to game and level design. Over the four years of university, I not only acquired knowledge and skills in user experience, interaction design, cinematography, editing and other areas but also gained in-depth understanding of game design, development, and project management. I`m looking forward to carve out a career in video game industry.
