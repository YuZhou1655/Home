About Me

Hello! My name is Yu Zhou and currently a student at University of Miami double major in Interactive Media and Motion Picture.

Initially, I was drawn to the immersive and evolving world of interactive media. I recognized its potential to engage audiences in unprecedented ways, fascinated by the intersection of technology and creativity. Throughout the years in college, I delved into courses that explored the diverse realms of user experience, physical computing, and interactive storytelling.

As I discovered a profound interest in the art of storytelling, intrigued by the power of visual narratives, I decided to pursue a double major in motion picture. I believed that a solid foundation in motion picture would not only enhance my understanding of narrative structure but also provide a unique perspective on translating stories into interactive experiences.

The synergy between interactive media and motion picture became evident. This interdisciplinary approach allowed me to explore the combination of traditional storytelling with interactive elements, laying the groundwork for my ultimate passion – game design.

With a comprehensive understanding of interactive media and a proficiency in visual storytelling, I made up my mind to pursue game design. While my skills in interactive media provided the technical expertise needed for crafting engaging and user-friendly game experiences, my motion picture knowledge enriched my ability to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant narratives within the games.

The combination of interactive media and motion picture expertise equips me with a skill set that is valuable in the continuously developing field of game design. I`m looking forward to carve out a career in video game industry.
